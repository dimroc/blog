Pundit for Roles on Rails

23 Oct 2014

Roles are almost always a requirement for a web app. There are many good options out there for Rails, with the old guard being CanCan and Rolify. But then I met this newcomer, Pundit, and its simplicity stole the show.

The Rails 3 goto was CanCan, and I loved it. CanCan worked great with Devise and helped encapsulate everything role related into Abilities.

Pundit encapsulates authorization and scoping through Policies, using pure ruby classes and amazingly intuitive convention over configuration.

This does mean that you will have to add admin (or whatever you need) columns manually, but the simplicity of it all will save you in the long run.
